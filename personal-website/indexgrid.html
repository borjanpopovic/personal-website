bio

Hi and welcome to my website. I am Borjan. I grew up in Frankfurt am Main, Germany. Here I finished school and studied economic science at the Frankfurt university followed by a period as an employee at my college. Later I lived in Berlin for a while. Here I went to university again to attend a collaborative interdisciplanary master proram by the UdK and TU Berlin. Before I could finish it I had to go back to Frankfurt. After a while of working at the uni administrative department again I found a new occupation as a caretaker at two schools and interpreter. In the beginning of 2020 i resigned as i had a job offer by an old school friend of mine. But because of the global events that followed that opportunity unfortunately didn't work out. After a few months of looking for jobs and some further potential education and training here I am at the neuefische web development bootcamp.
